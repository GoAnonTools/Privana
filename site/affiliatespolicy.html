Affiliate Program Policy

Clear rules, fair payouts, zero tolerance for fraud.

1) Definitions

  • Affiliate: A partner approved by Privana to promote our services.
  • Qualifying Event: The first successful subscription payment by a new customer referred by the Affiliate (after the free trial), not refunded or charged back during the lock period.
  • Lock Period: 15 calendar days following the initial subscription charge, used to verify legitimacy and refund/chargeback status.
  • Cookie Window: Last-click attribution within a default 60 days.

2) Payout Schedule (twice monthly)

We pay in two batches each month after the 15-day lock period:

Group A

Qualifying Events dated 1st–15th → paid on the 2nd of the following month.

Group B

Qualifying Events dated 16th–31st → paid on the 17th of the following month.

If a payout date falls on a weekend/holiday, we pay next business day. UTC used for cutoffs.

Examples: May 12 → June 2. May 28 → June 17.

3) Rates & Eligibility

  • Commission rates and tiers are shown in your Affiliate dashboard and may change prospectively.
  • Commissions apply only to new customers who complete a Qualifying Event. Self-referrals are not eligible.

    • Note: Recurring commissions may be offered on an invitation-only pilot basis at our discretion; we are not accepting inquiries.

  • Renewals/add-ons are commissionable only if explicitly stated in your dashboard.
  • Commissions are calculated on the net subscription amount (excluding taxes/fees/credits/discounts).

4) Anti-Fraud & Traffic Quality

  • Self-referrals, household referrals, fake/duplicate accounts.
  • Misleading claims, impersonation, or implying official partnership.
  • Cookie stuffing, forced clicks, adware/toolbars, malware, bots/automation.
  • Spam or non-compliant email marketing; no unsolicited outreach.
  • PPC brand bidding on restricted terms (“Privana”, misspellings, “coupon/discount” combos); no deceptive direct-to-site ads.
  • Unauthorized couponing/incentives; only Privana-approved codes.
  • Traffic from sanctioned regions or otherwise prohibited by law.

We may deny/reverse commissions or suspend accounts for violations or risk signals.

5) Tracking & Attribution

  • Use your unique link as provided; do not alter tracking parameters.
  • Last-click attribution within the cookie window (default 60 days).
  • Direct/organic checkout without a valid referral parameter is non-commissionable.
  • Server-side records govern in case of discrepancy.

6) Reversals, Refunds & Chargebacks

  • Orders refunded/charged-back within the 15-day lock are not commissionable.
  • Post-payout refund/chargeback may be clawed back against future earnings.
  • Suspected fraud may extend lock period or lead to permanent reversal/termination.

7) Payments

  • Payout methods: bank transfer or PayPal (others case-by-case).
  • Minimum payout threshold: $50. Balances roll over.
  • Transfer fees (if any) may be deducted.
  • KYC/tax forms may be required before payment.

8) Brand & Claims

  • Use the “Privana” name and assets as provided; do not alter the logo.
  • Keep claims accurate (no logs by design, Panama jurisdiction, PSK hardening). Avoid unverified claims.
  • Do not suggest endorsement beyond the affiliate relationship.

9) Changes & Contact

We may update this policy at any time; continued participation implies acceptance. Operated by GoAnon. Questions: affiliates@privana.com.
