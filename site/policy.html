Privacy Policy

Privana is designed around anonymous access. We do not ask for an email address, password, username, phone number, or real-world identity to create an account.

1. Who operates Privana

Privana is operated by GoAnon. References to “Privana”, “we”, “us”, or “our” mean the Privana service operated by GoAnon.

2. What we do not collect

  • No email address is required for anonymous account creation.
  • No password is created or stored.
  • No username, phone number, or identity document is required.
  • No browsing history, DNS query log, traffic content, or websites visited are logged by Privana.
  • No advertising tracker is required to use the authenticated Privana app.

3. What we process to run the service

To operate the service, Privana processes a minimal set of technical data:

  • Anonymous account number: a 16-digit account number used to log in.
  • Recovery code hash: the recovery code itself is shown once to the user; we store only a hash.
  • Trial state: trial start, expiry, and consumed status.
  • Device entries: device name, platform label, WireGuard public key, assigned VPN IP, and generated configuration template.
  • Security events: limited events such as failed login attempts, rate limiting, or suspicious activity, used to protect the service.
  • Session and CSRF data: technical data required to keep you logged in and protect forms/actions.

4. Recovery codes

Your recovery code is shown once. If you use it, Privana immediately rotates your account by issuing a fresh account number and a fresh recovery code. The previous account number and previous recovery code stop working.

5. WireGuard keys and VPN configuration

Privana is built around WireGuard. Client private keys are intended to be generated on the user device and should not be sent to Privana. The server needs the client public key to create a working VPN peer.

6. Payments and paid plans

If paid plans are enabled, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers. Privana does not need to store full card numbers on its own servers. Any payment-provider data is handled under the provider’s own terms and privacy policy.

7. Analytics

Privana does not require third-party analytics to operate the authenticated app. If privacy-friendly analytics are introduced on public marketing pages, they should be optional and described in the Cookie Policy.

8. Your responsibility

Because Privana does not use email/password recovery, you are responsible for saving your account number and recovery code. If both are lost, we may not be able to restore access.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact GoAnon through GoAnon.pro.
